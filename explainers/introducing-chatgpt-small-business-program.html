Introducing the ChatGPT for Small Business Program

A program to help entrepreneurs use AI as a force multiplier — training, in-person academies, ready-to-use guides, and partner integrations, all built around ChatGPT Work.

The idea in five lines

  • Small business owners have to be marketer, accountant, salesperson, operator, and strategist all at once — with a lean team and little time.
  • OpenAI is launching a ChatGPT for small businesses program to help owners be more productive and scale with ChatGPT Work.
  • It bundles four things: hands-on virtual training, in-person AI academies, ready-to-use guides, and small-business-specific agents & partners.
  • Partners include Dropbox, Shopify, Intuit, Slack, Atlassian, and Wix, with skills built for common small-business workflows.
  • ChatGPT Work — powered by GPT-5.6 — is available to businesses of any size today.
The concept

AI as a force multiplier for lean teams

The premise: small businesses start with people exceptional at a craft, but running the business demands far more than that expertise. OpenAI’s framing is that AI can extend individual expertise, add capacity, and give every owner access to world-class tools — so the many hats an owner wears get lighter.
