Introducing OpenAI Presence

A battle-tested enterprise product for putting AI agents to work across customer and internal workflows — pairing model reasoning with policies, guardrails, and escalation rules.

SourceOpenAI News
CategoryProduct · Company
ChannelsVoice & Chat
AvailabilityLimited GA

The idea in five lines

  • The enterprise challenge is no longer proving agents can work — it’s making them reliable enough for high-value production work.
  • OpenAI Presence deploys trusted agents that answer questions, use company systems, take approved actions, and escalate to people when needed.
  • Each deployment starts with one specific job and gets only the knowledge and system access that job requires.
  • The company sets the policies: what the agent can do, when it needs approval, and when a human takes over.
  • After launch, Codex reads production sessions and escalations and proposes updates teams can test and approve.
The concept

More than a model — a system for production

Agent behavior has to adapt as products, policies, and user behavior change. That takes more than a model: it needs the systems, evaluations, and deployment expertise to improve agents as conditions shift — without giving up control. Presence brings those components together.

The building blocks teams need to run agents in production ship together: policies and standard operating procedures, guardrails, approved actions, simulations, evaluation tools, and a Codex-powered improvement process. Companies decide what stays consistent across deployments — policies, evaluations, escalation rules — and what changes per workflow or channel.

Trust

Built for trust before, during, and after launch

Before

Test against reality

Simulations and graders check whether the agent reached the right outcome, followed policy, used tools correctly, and escalated when appropriate.

During

Guardrails intervene

Guardrails step in when an interaction moves outside the company’s boundaries, keeping the agent inside approved limits.

After

Codex-driven rework

Production signals reveal gaps; Codex investigates and suggests updates that teams test against production, then roll out under control.

Presence is designed to learn with you — improving as your business, customers, and employees evolve while the business stays in control. When a use case goes beyond what the product supports, OpenAI Forward Deployed Engineers and partners help bring it into production.

Availability

OpenAI Presence is available to eligible enterprise customers through a limited general-availability program, led by OpenAI Forward Deployed Engineers and select global systems integrators. Not yet self-serve.
