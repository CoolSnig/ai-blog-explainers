Introducing Workspace Intelligence

A secure system that gives Gemini a unified, real-time understanding of your apps, projects, and colleagues — so it can gather context and take action across your whole workday, not just answer questions.

SourceGoogle Workspace Blog
ProductGemini for Workspace
AuthorYulie Kwon Kim, VP Product
Reading time5 min

The idea in five lines

  • Most of us lose hours stitching together scattered info across tabs, emails, and chats before we even start the real work.
  • Workspace Intelligence understands the meaning and relationships across your Docs, Slides, Gmail, projects, and org knowledge — not just the raw data.
  • It automates three things: gathering the right information, knowing what matters right now, and personalizing output to your voice and style.
  • It shows up across Chat, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail, and Drive — and connects to outside tools like Asana, Jira, and Salesforce.
  • Enterprise-grade: your data isn’t used for ads or model training, with US/EU data residency and client-side encryption.
The concept

From a blank canvas to a system that understands your work

The pitch: your tools shouldn’t just be a canvas you fill in — they should understand your goal. Workspace Intelligence is a dynamic layer that grasps the semantic relationships between your content, active projects, collaborators, and company knowledge, then does the heavy lifting for you.

Figure 1 · What Workspace Intelligence automates
🔍

Information gathering

Breaks down context walls to pull together everything you need the moment you want to act.

🧠

Situational awareness

Uses Gemini reasoning to know what’s most important right now, so you never miss an action item.

✎

True personalization

Learns your work style, voice, and formatting so every output sounds authentically like you.

It turns your scattered emails, chats, and files into a cohesive knowledge graph that both your colleagues and Gemini can draw on.
Where it shows up

The same intelligence across your workday

Chat

Ask Gemini in Chat

A single command line for work: a daily briefing of tasks and urgent threads, plus skills that generate docs and slides, schedule meetings, and find files — even reaching into Asana, Jira, and Salesforce.

Docs · Sheets · Slides

Blank page to brilliance

Turns your emails, chats, and files into formatted drafts in your voice; builds and edits spreadsheets from natural language; adds data-grounded infographics in Docs; and creates full, editable decks in one shot.

Gmail

See what matters most

AI Inbox gives a streamlined view of what’s important, and AI Overviews in search synthesize answers across threads instead of making you dig.

Drive

An active knowledge base

AI Overviews and Ask Gemini surface insights across files, and Drive Projects centrally organize files and emails so colleagues — and Gemini — have full context.

Trust

The AI-first trifecta: agents, context, and security

Google’s framing is that AI’s value comes not just from reasoning, but from understanding context and completing tasks — done securely. Workspace Intelligence runs on the same governed infrastructure as the rest of Workspace.

Your data is yours

No ads, no outside training

Content generated or used by your people or your agents isn’t reviewed by humans, used for ads, or used to train AI models outside Workspace without your permission.

Controls

Residency & encryption

Lock data processing and storage to the US or EU (more countries coming), and use client-side encryption to authoritatively deny access to your most sensitive data — even from Google.

Instead of spending your day bridging apps and projects, the system does it for you — and the output already sounds like you.

Availability

Workspace Intelligence rolls out with admin controls to manage its use across an organization; detailed rollout timing is tracked on the Workspace Updates blog. It was announced alongside a wider set of Workspace news at Google Cloud Next ’26.
