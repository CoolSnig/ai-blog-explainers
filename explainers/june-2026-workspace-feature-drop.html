June Workspace Drops: New AI Features for Drive, Sheets & Gmail

The idea in four lines

  • June’s Workspace drop is a set of AI features aimed at keeping your work organized and moving.
  • Three headliners: Organize my files in Drive, fix formula errors with Gemini in Sheets, and an improved Help me write in Gmail.
  • You stay in control — e.g. approve Drive’s filing suggestions one-by-one or in bulk.

What’s new

Three AI drops for everyday work

Figure 1 · June 2026 Workspace drops
📁
Drive

Organize my files

Instead of dragging files into folders, Drive suggests the best folder for loose files (or creates one). Review file-by-file or approve in bulk.

📊
Sheets

Fix formula errors

Hit an error, click ‘fix’, and Gemini analyzes surrounding data to find the syntax or logic fault, explains it, and fixes the formula inline.

✍
Gmail

Improved Help me write

Draft as usual, then use the prompt bar to make specific edits — add a missing detail or a deadline — and Gmail updates the draft instantly.

Sheets

28 more languages

Build and edit spreadsheets with Gemini in Spanish, Japanese, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, Korean, and more.
